Boston Terrier

The Boston Terrier is nicknamed the "American Gentleman" because it is one of the first American dog breeds.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Weight: 15 - 25 lbs
  • Average Height: 15 - 17 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 13 - 15 years

The Boston Terrier is the perfect dog. They are very fun to play with and have amazing personalities. Although they could be hyper, you will find that they are great to have around and perfect for families.
